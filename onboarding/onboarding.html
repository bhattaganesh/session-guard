Welcome to Session Guard

Session Guard signs you out of your personal accounts whenever you close Chrome — without closing your open tabs. Your work accounts are never touched.

How it works

1

You close Chrome. Session Guard clears the session cookies for your selected accounts.

2

Next time you open Chrome, your tabs restore normally — but protected accounts require re-login.

3

You log back in once. Your password manager fills in your credentials automatically.

Which accounts do you want to protect?

You can add or remove services later in Settings.
